Step 1: Define Your Real Estate Goals

Before you start searching, take time to identify what you want from your Realtor and your transaction.

Ask yourself:

  • How hands-on do you want your agent to be?

By knowing what you’re looking for, you’ll be able to evaluate potential Realtors based on experience that matches your situation.

Step 3: Interview Multiple Realtors

Never choose based on reputation alone. Interview at least two or three Realtors to get a feel for their approach and compatibility.

Smart questions to ask:

  • How long have you worked in the Menifee real estate market?

  • What type of properties do you specialize in?

  • Can you share examples of recent successful deals?

  • How will you keep me updated throughout the process?

  • What’s your marketing strategy for my home (if selling)?

You’ll quickly discover who’s confident, knowledgeable, and committed to your goals.

Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I know if I’ve chosen the right Realtor?
You’ll know you’ve found the right Realtor when they communicate clearly, understand your goals, and make you feel confident about each step of the process.

2. Should I work with a local Realtor in Menifee?

3. How do Realtors charge for their services?
Most work on commission, typically 5–6% of the sale price, split between buyer and seller agents. Discuss fees upfront to avoid surprises.

4. Can I change Realtors if I’m not satisfied?
Yes. If you’ve signed a contract, you may need to review the cancellation terms, but you have the right to find a better fit.
